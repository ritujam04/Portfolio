Paper Trunk


Description: The “Paper Trunk” is an innovative application aimed at promoting paper recycling and reducing waste by incentivizing users to recycle waste or unused leftover pages. The application will provide a convenient and user-friendly platform for individuals, businesses, and educational institutions to recycle their unused or discarded paper pages effectively. By encouraging responsible paper waste management, the Paper Trunk seeks to contribute significantly to environmental conservation and sustainability.

Key Features: 1. User Login (Profile and Registration). 2. Shopkeeper SignUp (Registration Details). 3. Users selecting a desired/required option. 4. The shopkeeper details with contact no., address, photograph of the shop will be displayed as per the selected option by the respective user.
